I am Kritika, the girl who admires
her own humour

I'm a Civil engineering undergraduate from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati with a minor in Product Design. Having been a creative person at crossroads with technology, my output has always been Design; be it the aesthetics in a presentation, finding solution to a real-life problem or the design of a structure. My design journey has been greatly influenced by this quote:

"Creativity is allowing yourself to make mistakes. Design is knowing which ones to keep."

-Scott Adams

From being a shy student at school to still being an introvert, I have grown to realize that those earnest '2-extra-marks-for-good-presentation' presentations I made in school happens to be my forte.

I happen to overthink and optimize on how can I make the experience seamless for the other person, be it organizing a table for dinner, throwing car keys from the balcony or planning a trip.

Even though I lacked a formal education in Design, my passion to understand user experience and enhance it has kept me afloat in this field.

Apart from design, I am into cooking and baking (currently trying to perfect the recipe of vegetarian quiche). A flaneur, I like to explore places. A connoisseur of art, I can explain any abstract painting with my vivid imagination. A very resourceful person, you will find everything in my bag and a hoarder, I collect every little souvenir I find. My music taste transcends the demarcation of genres, yet I'll say, I love The Lumineers. And movies! Instead of popcorn, I sit with a litre of buttermilk XP (I know, very weird)
